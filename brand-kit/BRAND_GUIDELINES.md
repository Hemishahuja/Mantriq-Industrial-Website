# Mantriq Industrial - Brand Guidelines

This document outlines the visual identity for Mantriq Industrial.

## 1. Vision & Voice
- **Identity**: Reliable, authoritative, high-output industrial machining.
- **Voice**: Precise, technical, and responsive.

## 2. Color Palette
The brand uses a "Modern Industrial" palette designed for high contrast and technical clarity.

| Name | HSL | Hex (approx) | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Space Cadet** | `221 39% 11%` | `#111827` | Deep Backgrounds, Global Text |
| **Ocean Steel** | `221 83% 45%` | `#1D4ED8` | Primary Branding, Call-to-Actions |
| **Safety Orange** | `24 95% 40%` | `#EC4899` | Accent, Highlights, Status |
| **Polar Ice** | `210 20% 98%` | `#F8FAFC` | Light Backgrounds, Contrast Sections |

## 3. Typography
We utilize high-legibility sans-serif typefaces to reflect our precision standards.

- **Display (Headings)**: `Outfit`
  - Style: Bold/Extrabold
  - Tracking: Tight (for a "solid" feel)
- **Body Content**: `Inter`
  - Style: Regular/Medium
  - Feature: `rlig`, `calt` enabled for coding-style precision.

## 4. Logo Guidelines
- **The Dot**: The orange dot in `MANTRIQ.` represents precision and completion. It should always be present.
- **Tagline**: The "Industrial" tagline uses wide tracking (`0.3em`) to symbolize scale and global presence.
